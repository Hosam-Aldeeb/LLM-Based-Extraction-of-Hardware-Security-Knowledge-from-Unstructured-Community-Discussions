On a modern Intel platform:
1. EC reads the power button going low
2. EC verifies that all power good signals are present
3. EC turns on +1.8 V core logic for the PCH
4. EC waits for the PCH to tell it that its power wells are stable
5. EC asserts `RSMRST` (resume reset) to the PCH
6. PCH replies with `PLTRST` (platform reset, final high-level signal) and takes it from there, reading the main BIOS ROM and starting the CPU
